Motion in One Dimension . Top .

$$v = \dfrac{\Delta x}{\Delta t} = m \cdot s^{-1}$$
$$speed = \dfrac{d}{\Delta t} = m \cdot s^{-1}$$
$$a = \dfrac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = m \cdot s^{-2}$$

$$v_f = v_i + at$$
$$\Delta x = \dfrac{v_i + v_f}{2}t$$
$$\Delta x = v_i t + \dfrac{1}{2}at^2$$
$$v^2_f = v^2_i + 2a\Delta x$$
